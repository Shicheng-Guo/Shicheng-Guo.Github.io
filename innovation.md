<p class="lead">Innovation, for me, means building the methods and platforms that didn't exist yet — then using them to answer questions the field couldn't answer before.</p>

<h2 class="archive__subtitle">Areas of Innovation</h2>

<div class="highlights">
  <div class="hl-card">
    <i class="fa-solid fa-flask-vial"></i>
    <h3>Novel Methods</h3>
    <p>Original statistical-genetics approaches — including a gene-based recessive diplotype scan that discovered a new disease gene (FGF6).</p>
  </div>
  <div class="hl-card">
    <i class="fa-solid fa-droplet"></i>
    <h3>Liquid Biopsy</h3>
    <p>Methylation haplotype blocks enabling non-invasive tissue-of-origin mapping and early detection from cell-free DNA.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="hl-card">
    <i class="fa-solid fa-robot"></i>
    <h3>AI-Driven Discovery</h3>
    <p>Machine-learning and multi-omic integration platforms that prioritize and validate drug targets at biobank scale.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="hl-card">
    <i class="fa-solid fa-lightbulb"></i>
    <h3>Translational Platforms</h3>
    <p>Reproducible data-science infrastructure that converts human-genetic evidence into go/no-go program decisions.</p>
  </div>
</div>
