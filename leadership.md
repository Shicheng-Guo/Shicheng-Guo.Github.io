<p class="lead">As Senior Director of Translational Genetics &amp; Data Science, I lead the strategy, people, and platforms that turn human-genetic evidence into therapeutic programs.</p>

<p>My role spans the full arc of translational science: setting the genetics and data-science strategy for target discovery and biomarker development, building cross-functional teams that bridge computational and experimental biology, and partnering across discovery, clinical, and regulatory functions to move RNA-based therapeutics forward with confidence.</p>
