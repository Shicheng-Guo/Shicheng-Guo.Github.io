From the human genome
to the next medicine.

I build the computational and genetic evidence that decides which targets become drugs — and which patients they can help.

Trained as a statistical and computational geneticist, I have spent over a decade at the intersection of genomics, epigenetics, and machine learning across leading academic and clinical research institutions — Fudan University, UT Health Houston, UC San Diego, and the Marshfield Clinic Research Institute & University of Wisconsin–Madison.

Today, as Senior Director of Translational Genetics & Data Science at Arrowhead Pharmaceuticals, I direct human-genetics-driven target discovery and biomarker strategy for RNA-based therapeutics — connecting population-scale data, functional genomics, and clinical insight to accelerate precision medicine.
