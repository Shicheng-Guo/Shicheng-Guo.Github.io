* how do I prune second-degree-related samples?  —rel-cutoff is obsolete and see [—king-cutoff](https://www.cog-genomics.org/plink/2.0/distance#make_king)

```
plink2 —bfile ... —king-cutoff 0.088 —make-bed
```
